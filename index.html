Blueprint

Blueprint was a 3-day flagship event conducted by ISOI-VIT from 20th February to 22nd February 2019 in TT-gallery 001. The event covered topics such as: - Data Science & ML, Cloud & DevOps, and 3D printing on the 3 respective days. The speakers for the event were: Mr. A R Janardhanan, Mr. Milind Kar Ray and Mr. Vikas Garg, who spoke on their respective topics on these respective days. The event attracted a large crowd of 250 students from different branches. The event also proved to be a window for the budding engineers to the world of technology. Each day of the event was followed by giving off a memento to the guest speakers as a token of appreciation. The duration of the event was of 2 hrs during which the crowd was excellently managed by the coordinators and volunteers.

  • Date: 20/02/2019 TO 22/02/2019
  • Category: Tech-Event

MALARIA DETECTION USING TRANSFER LEARNING

TRANSFER LEARNING

The main objective of this project is to give patients the instant result whether they have malaria or not. Our product is easy to use and does not require any medical knowledge therefore can be used by anyone. It will also ensure contactless checkup during this COVID-19 pandemic and reduce some workload for the doctors.Using transfer learning models such as (VGG16, VGG19, Inception etc.) and adequate dataset, the accuracy of prediction can be boosted to a great extent and reducing the timing of medical tests to a matter of seconds. This project could be a great step to control a disease like malaria.
